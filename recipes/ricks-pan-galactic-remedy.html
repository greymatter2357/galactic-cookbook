Rick's Pan Galactic Remedy

Description:

This unique cocktail is a twist on the classic Penicillin cocktail, with an added intergalactic flair that Rick Sanchez himself would love..

Ingredients:

  1. 2 oz of blended Scotch whisky
  2. 3/4 oz of fresh lemon juice
  3. 3/4 oz of honey syrup (1:1 ratio of honey and water)
  4. 1/4 oz of ginger syrup (1:1 ratio of ginger juice and sugar)
  5. 1/4 oz of Blue Curaçao liqueur
  6. 2 dashes of absinthe

Steps:

  1. To make the cocktail, combine all ingredients in a shaker with and shake vigorously.
  2. Strain the mixture over fresh ice in a rocks glass.
  3. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a sprig of fresh thyme.
  4. Allow four litres of Fallian marsh gas to bubble through it, in ory of all those happy hikers who have died of pleasure in the shes of Fallia.
  5. This bold and complex cocktail is the perfect remedy for any space adventure or existential crisis like Rick and Morty often face.
